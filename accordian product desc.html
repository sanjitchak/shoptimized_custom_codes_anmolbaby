• Description

    The Easy Burgundy Soft Structured Carrier is complete in its own way to showcase elegance, subtleness and trend. With this carrier, you can surely say Burgundy is the new Red!

    This is a budget carrier, with the body panel, waistband and shoulder straps made of Grey 100% cotton, and contrasting accents of handwoven wrap fabric on the thigh padding. The cotton fabric ensures sturdiness and durability, enabling this carrier to withstand more weight and endure more pressure, for a longer period of time. Lightweight and compact, it’s a steal deal.

  • Specifications

    Made from (Fabric): 100% cotton fabric, with handwoven wrap used on thigh padding.

    Ideal for age: 4 months (2 months with insert) - 3 years

    Ideal for weight: 7 kg (5 kg with insert) - 20 kg

  • Special Features

    Extremely sturdy and durable.
    Can be used for both front and back carries.
    World’s best quality Duraflex buckles used.
    3 point safety buckle on waistbelt to prevent accidental opening.
    Waistbelt also includes elastic belt loop for additional security.
    Soft padded thigh cushions for baby’s comfort.
    In-built hood for protective covering, enables discreet nursing.
    Easily accessible pocket on waistband.

  • Important

    We do not recommend back carry until your baby has strong and consistent head, neck, and torso control, and can push him/herself independently into, and remain in, a seated position on a consistent basis

  • Wash Care

    Spot cleaning when needed.
    Machine-washable.
    Cover buckles in socks & carrier in a pillowcase to avoid damage.
    Use mild detergent. Do not bleach.
    Dry in shade with no direct exposure to sunlight.
